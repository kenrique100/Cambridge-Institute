High School - Sciences Program

Cambridge Empowered Bilingual Vocational Center, Kombe Village Q7, Mbanga Subdivision, Moungo Department, Littoral Region, Cameroon

Our High School Sciences program provides students with a rigorous bilingual education in the core scientific disciplines. The curriculum is designed to prepare students for university studies and careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields.

The program covers advanced topics in:

  • Physics - Classical mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism
  • Chemistry - Organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry
  • Biology - Molecular biology, genetics, ecology
  • Mathematics - Calculus, algebra, statistics
  • Computer Science - Programming, algorithms, data structures

Students benefit from:
  • Well-equipped science laboratories
  • Experienced bilingual faculty
  • Practical research projects
  • Preparation for national and international examinations
  • Career guidance and university placement support

The program duration is three years, leading to the Cameroon GCE Advanced Level examinations. Graduates of our science program have gone on to study medicine, engineering, computer science, and other STEM fields at universities in Cameroon and abroad.
