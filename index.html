Welcome to cambridge Empowered Bilingual Vocational Training Center

Cambridge Empowered Bilingual Vocational Center is a comprehensive learning institution offering nursery, primary, secondary, high school, and vocational training programs. Located in the serene village of Kombe, within the Mbanga Subdivision of the Littoral Region in Cameroon, our school serves as a beacon of knowledge and opportunity for the local community and beyond. Set at an altitude of 102 meters in the Moungo department, Kombe’s peaceful environment fosters focused learning, while our bilingual approach prepares students for both national and international success.

High School – Sciences

Advanced studies in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics preparing students for scientific and technical careers.

High School – Arts

In-depth studies of Literature, History, Philosophy, Geography, Economics, French, and Languages that foster analytical skills, cultural awareness, and creative expression.

Introduction to Computer Engineering

Hands-on training in hardware basics, computer systems, and foundational engineering concepts for beginners.

Computer Web Designing

Creative and technical training in modern website design, development tools, and user experience principles.

Building & Construction

Practical instruction in construction, safety, and building fundamentals with hands-on training using sun-dried bricks and zinc sheets. This course teaches sustainable methods and equips students to build safe, durable structures.

Aquaculture – Catfish Production

Specialized training in sustainable fish farming practices with a focus on catfish breeding and production.

Introduction to Electrical Engineering

Fundamental skills in electrical systems, wiring, safety, and basic electronics for beginners.
